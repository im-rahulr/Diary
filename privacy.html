Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

Your Privacy Matters

At Personal Diary App, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our web application.

Important: The Personal Diary App stores all your diary entries locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your diary content to any server.

Information Collection

Local Storage

All diary entries, including text, timestamps, and categories, are stored exclusively on your device using browser local storage. This means:

  • Your diary entries remain on your device and are never transmitted to our servers
  • No one but you can access your entries (unless they have access to your device)
  • Your entries persist between sessions on the same device
  • Clearing your browser's local storage or cache will delete your entries

Usage Data

We do not collect any usage data, analytics, or tracking information. There are no cookies, tracking pixels, or other tracking technologies used in our application.

Data Security

Since all your diary entries are stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on the security of your device and browser. We recommend that you:

  • Use a secure, password-protected device
  • Keep your operating system and browser up-to-date
  • Consider making periodic backups of your entries if they're important to you
  • Log out of shared devices after using the application

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
